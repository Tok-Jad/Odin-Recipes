Turkey Sandwich

Description

Turkey Sandwich with Garlic Bread and chopped Onions

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Turkey
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix Butter, Crushed Garlic, Salt, and Pepper together to make the Garlic Butter
  2. Spread the Butter on the Bread and toast it for 10 minutes
  3. Chop the Onions and start frying it in a heated pan with Olive Oil
  4. Add Salt to the Onions and stir gently
  5. Start cooking the Turkey in another heated pan with Olive Oil and add Salt and Pepper
  6. When the Turkey is halfway cooked add Cheese on top
  7. When the Bread is finished toasting, add the Turkey and put the Onions on top
